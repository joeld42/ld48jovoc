Introduction

The assign page will show you a list of 10 or so game titles that need genres assigned. Pick any one of them and you will be brought to the game assign page.

The compo page on the LDJAM site will be displayed in a frame below the genres. Read the description and check the two genres from the list that best fit the game. After picking, choose one of the actions:

  • UPDATE - will save the changes to the genre and remain on this page.
  • Save and Pick - Will save the genres and return to pick another game.
  • Save and Get Another - Will save the genres and give you another game to assign at random.

Tips and Advice

  • Always prefer the author's intent. If the author(s) state the genres for the game, always go with that, even if the game seems to better fit a different genre.
  • If a genre is not present on the list, choose the closest one you can. For example, there's no "Survival" genre on my list, but these often fit under "Farming", "Horror" or "Hunting".
  • If a genre really doesn't fit anything else, assign it to "Other". Try to avoid this catch-all category but it's better to put something there than someplace clearly wrong. For example, a "Drawing" + "Racing" game could be filed under "Other" + "Racing".
  • Many entries are incomplete, placeholders, or don't have enough information in the description. In this case, assign them the special genre "Don't Know". Games assigned to this genre will not be included in the data, and will be set aside to categorize later. Don't mix "Dont Know" with other genres.
  • Some games are really a single genre, for example the author might have combined incompatible settings or themes instead of game mechanics, or just didn't want to follow the theme too closely. That's fine! If this is the case, assign them just one genre. These entries appear along the diagonal in the matrix.
  • Some games are more than two genres, or even trying to fit a whole bunch of genres in. If it really does fit all those genres, go ahead and assign it more than two. Don't assign more than four genres as that will weight the game too much in the matrix.

NOTE: The data displayed on the front page only updates every 10 minutes or so. You won't see your changes reflected immediately, but they should show up if you check back in 10 minutes.
